Top 10 Cyber Security Tips

Choose unique. passwords. Don't follow links or open attachments from untrusted sources. Never leave your devices unattended. Keep your operating systems up to date. Set up multifactor authentication on your devices. Avoid using your work email on public facing internet websites. Avoid the use of public wi-fi especially to conduct business. Stay smart with social media. Check bank statements/quotes regularly. Make sure to familiarise yourself with your organisation's acceptable use policy or speak to your local IT Security contact.
